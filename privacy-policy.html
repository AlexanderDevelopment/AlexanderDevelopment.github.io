Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2026

This privacy policy describes a static HTML website hosted on GitHub Pages, with a third-party contact form provider, external social media links, and Discord content that stays blocked until you choose to load it.

2. Hosting and server logs

When you visit this website, the hosting provider may automatically process technical information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, referrer URL, requested pages, date and time of access, and server status codes. This data is required to deliver the website, maintain security, and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Contact form

The contact form on this website submits your name, email address, and message over an encrypted HTTPS connection to Formspree, Inc., a third-party form processing provider, which accepts the submission and forwards the inquiry to our mailbox. The data is processed only to receive and respond to your inquiry.

Formspree may also process technical metadata such as IP address, browser details, referrer, and access times for service operation, delivery security, and spam prevention. According to the provider, this processing may include transfers to the United States and other jurisdictions. For more information, please review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy and its security overview at formspree.io/security.

4. Discord content

The Discord community preview on this website is disabled by default. It is loaded only after you explicitly choose to enable it. When you do so, your browser connects to Discord services, including Discord's public invite API and Discord's content delivery network, to load the public server snapshot and icon. In that process, Discord may receive technical information such as your IP address, browser details, and request metadata, and may use cookies or similar technologies under its own policies.

To remember your choice, this website stores your Discord content preference in your browser's local storage on your device. This storage is used only to remember whether Discord content should stay blocked or may be loaded automatically on future visits from the same browser.

5. External links and media

This website links to Steam pages, Patreon, X/Twitter, TikTok, Reddit, and may load game artwork from Steam static content delivery domains. When external resources are loaded or external links are opened, the respective third-party provider may receive technical information from your browser.

6. Cookies, browser storage and analytics

This website does not intentionally use its own analytics tools, advertising trackers, or user accounts. However, the external contact form provider may use technical cookies or comparable security mechanisms when you submit the contact form. If you choose to load Discord content, Discord may also use cookies or similar technologies in connection with its own services. In addition, this website stores your Discord content preference locally in your browser so that your consent choice can be remembered on this device.

7. Legal basis

Technical server log processing is based on the legitimate interest of operating a secure and functional website. Contact requests are processed to respond to your message and, where applicable, to take steps prior to entering into a contract. Discord content is loaded only after your consent. The storage of your Discord content preference is based on that consent and on the necessity of remembering your selected privacy setting on this device.

8. Data retention

Data temporarily processed by the contact form provider is subject to that provider's own retention rules and technical operations.

Server logs are retained according to the hosting provider’s standard retention period. Contact emails are retained only as long as needed to process the request or as required by statutory retention obligations.

Your Discord content preference remains stored in your browser until you change the choice on the website, clear your browser storage, or use a different browser or device.

9. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to request access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection to processing. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority.

10. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the website changes, for example if analytics, embedded videos, a backend contact form, newsletter tools, or additional third-party services are added.
